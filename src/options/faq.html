Zinc Instant

Instructions

Signing Up

  1. Create an account with Zinc Instant on the settings page. If you already have an account, log in.
  2. Enter or update your billing and shipping information.

Placing Orders

  1. When you are on a page with an item you can purchase, the extension icon will turn from into . This means Zinc Instant is active on that page!
  2. Select the quantity of the item you want, as well as the size, color, and other dimensions.
  3. Click on the icon to bring up a confirmation popup. If you are ready to place the order, enter your password and click "Confirm Purchase".
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email within the next hour. It's now that easy to place an order online.

FAQ

Why should I use Instant?
Ever get tired of entering your credit card information or your shipping address when shopping online? Want to skip the lengthy checkout process? Want to make impulse buys with two clicks? Instant is for you.
How does it work?
When you submit an order, we use the Zinc API to automatically place it for you with the appropriate retailer.
How much will I pay?
Instant is free -- you are charged only for the price of the item, plus tax and shipping if applicable.
How do you secure my information?
We use industry standard, PCI compliant infrastructure to store your credit card and address information.
What if I want to change the quantity for an item?
Just select the quantity of the item you want like you would normally. Note that the price in the confirmation popup won't reflect the new quantity.
What if I want to buy more than one item?
For now, Instant only supports purchasing a single item at a time.
I'm on a product page, but Instant isn't lighting up. What do I do?
We're rapidly adding support for more retailers -- if we don't yet support your favorite online retailer, email us and let us know!
Who are you?
We're three MIT grads looking to change the landscape of online shopping.
