Zinc Instant

Settings

Zinc Instant allows you to buy anything from any online retailer in seconds.

Signing Up

  1. Go to the Options page of the extension.
  2. Create an account with Zinc Instant. Order confirmations will come to this email, and you will use this password to confirm purchases.
  3. Enter your billing and shipping information. We store this in our secure, PCI compliant infrastructure.

Placing Orders

  1. When you are on a page with an item you can purchase, the extension icon will turn from into . This means Zinc Instant is active on that page!
  2. Select the quantity of the item you want, as well as the size, color, and other dimensions.
  3. Click on the icon to bring up a confirmation popup. If you are ready to place the order, enter your password and click "Confirm Purchase".
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email within the next hour. It's now that easy to place an order online.
